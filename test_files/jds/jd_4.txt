JOB TITLE: Data Analyst

LOCATION: Hyderabad / Remote

JOB SUMMARY
We are looking for a Data Analyst to extract insights from large datasets and support data-driven decision-making.

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Analyze datasets and generate insights
- Build dashboards and reports using BI tools
- Perform data cleaning and preprocessing
- Use statistical methods and models
- Collaborate with business teams

REQUIREMENTS
- 1–3 years experience in data analysis
- Strong knowledge of Python and SQL
- Experience with Pandas, NumPy
- Familiarity with Power BI or Tableau

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
- Experience with machine learning basics
- Knowledge of data pipelines
- Exposure to big data tools
